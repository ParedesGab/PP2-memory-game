The Galaxy Memory Game!

The AI memory game consists of 16 cards featuring 8 unique pairs of pictures.At the start, all the cards are placed face down.

On each turn, you select and flip over two cards of your choice. If the pictures on the cards match, they remain face up. If not, they are flipped back face down.

The game continues until all the pairs have been matched.
